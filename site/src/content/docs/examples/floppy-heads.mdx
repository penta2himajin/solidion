## Controls

- **Click / Space** — flap (fly upward)
- Avoid the drive heads and the ground

## Highlights

- **Gravity physics**: Velocity accumulates via `GRAVITY * dt`, capped at `MAX_VEL`
- **Object pooling**: 4 head pairs are recycled — deactivated off-screen left, respawned on the right
- **Composite visuals**: Floppy disk built from 4 rectangles (body, slider, label, hub), all rotate together
- **Idle animation**: Bob with `Math.sin(t * 2.5) * 8` in "ready" phase
